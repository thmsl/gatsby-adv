This will allow individuals to store, control and update their passed test credentials for dissemination to businesses, organizations, groups and events.</span>\r\n\r\n<span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">Blockpass COVID-19 testing verification process...</span>\r\n<ul>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the COVID-19 testing service is run by an authorised clinic who does the test.</span></li>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the clinic or organization doing the test can operate their service via the Blockpass KYC Connect dashboard.</span></li>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the patient creates their digital identity profile via Blockpass’ gateway and then easily sends their digital identity profile to the clinic service, onboarding instantly - with the degree of identity document authentication and KYC verification that the clinic or testing customer requires.</span></li>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the individual takes COVID-19 test.</span></li>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the clinic will issue a COVID-19 certificate to the individual’s profile if they have taken the test and prove to be clear of infection.</span></li>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the user can prove they have taken the test by forwarding on the digital certificate that is stored in their digital identity profile.</span></li>\r\n \t<li style=\"font-weight: 400;\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">the user can update their COVID-19 certificate validity on a regular basis, by testing again and getting a new certificate issued.</span></li>\r\n</ul>\r\n<span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">This will not guarantee that individuals are immune to COVID-19 or are non-contagious, but it serves as a useful baseline for organizations that want to mitigate the risk level to their workforce or membership.</span>\r\n\r\n<span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">If you are an organization, medical clinic or testing service interested in the above service, please sign up to our console here </span><a href=\"http://console.blockpass.org\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">console.blockpass.org</span></a><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\"> and send email to </span><a href=\"mailto:support@blockpass.org\"><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\">support@blockpass.org</span></a><span style=\"font-weight: 400;\"> with subject “COVID-19 PASS”.</span>"
